And you can brag too

People ask me how they can learn to do what I do.

  • Find a boring job that you want to replace with a computer.
  • Start with a small part of that job.
  • Unsophisticated programs can get you pretty far.
  • Use existing software libraries so you don't have to write your own
  • If you can't get the data you want, come up with an alternative.
